PARENTS' DAY


Each year the Cadets display their talents to the world on their Parents day. Parents and guests are invited to watch the cadets display their different talents.Prizes are also distributed to the cadets who have excelled in academics or extra curricular activities through the year.




The 53rd Parents Day was held on November 29, 2018. Maj Gen Asad Nawaz Khan GOC 9 Div Kohat graced the function as Chief Guest. On arrival in the College, the Chief Guest was escorted to the Parade Square by the College Riding Squad. The College Principal, Brig (Retd) Inam ul Haq received the Chief Guest. The Chief Guest met the Faculty and Military Staff. He reviewed the Guard of Honour and was impressed by the excellent March Past of the cadets.
