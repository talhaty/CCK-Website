ADMISSION AND ENTRANCE


The rules and regulations for admission in the college are given as under: The administration has the right to alter these rules without prior notification and warnings.

1- Candidates for admission in the College should be:
   (a) Between 12 and 14 years on the 1st April of the year of admission.
   (b) Have passed class VII ( Standard IV ) at the time of admission.

2- Candidates studying 7th & 8th classes may apply for Entrance Examination provided they satisfy the conditions mentioned above. Students studying in 9th or 10th class are not eligible to take this examination.

3- The subjects for the Entrance Examination are :-
   (a) Urdu / Islamiyat
   (b) English
   (c) Mathematics / Intelligence.

4- Particulars regarding the Entrance Examination, interviews, etc. are advertised in the press every year in September and October. The centres for examination are Rawalpindi/Islamabad, Lahore, Peshawar, Mardan, Abbottabad, Chakdara, Kohat, D.I. Khan, Bannu, Karachi, Quetta.

5- Admission to the College is decided on the basis of the results of:
   (a) The Entrance Examination
   (b) Medical Test
   (c) An Interview by the Principal

6- Registration fee for Entrance Examination is Rs. 1600/- ( non-refundable) and Admission form downloaded from Internet submit Rs. 2200/-(non-refundable).

7- A selected candidate is admitted only if he produces a certificate of good character and a school leaving certificate from his previous recognized school/institution.
